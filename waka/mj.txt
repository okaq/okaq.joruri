mahjong tile symbolism

Suits:
char suit [1-9]
bamboo suit [1-9]
wheel suit [1-9]

flowers - plum, orchid, bamboo, mum
seasons - spring, summer, autumn, winter
winds - east, south, west, north
dragons - red, green, white

Deck:
36 bamboos (4 sets) = axis of earth
36 circles (4 sets) = wheel of heaven
36 chars (4 sets) = changes in life of man
16 simple honors (4 sets of winds)
12 superior honors (4 sets of dragons)
8 supreme honors (1 set each flowers, seasons)
